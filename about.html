About


statGNV lets you explore the City of Gainesville's data through interactive charts, graphs, and maps. You can now easily understand complex city data and track important trends in areas such as public safety, basic city services, human services, economic development, and others.

statGNV is continuously being refined as more and better data becomes available. Please share with us any questions, comments or recommendations you may have about statGNV. Your feedback help us make this tool better for our Open Data users like you. You can email us at designGNV@CityofGainesville.org or by clicking the Contact Us link at the bottom of this page to send us feedback.
