Fire Responses

About: This dashboard... Scroll to the bottom of the page to find a link to the raw data available on dataGNV, the City of Gainesville's Open Data Portal.

How to use: The dashboard and map are interactive. Use the slider on top to filter by date range. You can also filter fire responses by Month Over Time, by Type of Response, or Year by clicking on the bar graphs or line graph. To select multiple, use ctrl + click on Windows or cmd + click on Mac. Click again to de-select. Hovering over the graphs will give you more information about the data.
